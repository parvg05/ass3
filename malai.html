DELHI"S SPECIALITY


INGREDIENTS

6/8 soya chaap
1 cup cream
½ cup yogurt
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp chaat masala
Butter
Salt

PREP TIME:15 minutes (+ 1 hrs marination)

STEPS

Mix marinade ingredients.
Coat chaap and marinate.
Grill or pan-fry in butter.
Cook leftover marinade.
Add chaap and coat well.

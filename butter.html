FOR THE RICH INDIAN CULTURE


INGREDIENTS

500g chicken
1 cup yogurt
2 tbsp butter
1 cup tomato puree
1 onion
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp chili powder
½ cup cream
Salt

PREP TIME:20 minutes (+ 1 hrs marination)

STEPS

Marinate chicken for 1 hour.
Cook or grill chicken.
Sauté onion and ginger-garlic in butter.
Add tomato puree and cook.
Add chicken and simmer.
Finish with cream and spices.

A EASY GOING RECIPE FOR CHOCOLATE CAKE LOVERS!!!

INGREDIENTS

1½ cups all-purpose flour
1 cup sugar
½ cup cocoa powder
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
2 eggs
½ cup milk
½ cup oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
½ cup hot water

PREP TIME-15 MINS

STEPS

Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a cake tin.
Mix dry ingredients.
Add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla and mix.
Stir in hot water.
Bake for 30/35 minutes.
Cool and serve

CHECK FOR ALLERGIES BEFORE HAND

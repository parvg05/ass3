THE FRENCH


INGREDIENTS

1 zucchini
1 eggplant
1 bell pepper
2 tomatoes
1 onion
2 tbsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves
Salt & pepper
Herbs (thyme/basil)

PREP TIME:20 minutes (+ 30 mins cooking)

STEPS

Thinly slice all vegetables evenly.
Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
Add tomatoes to form a base sauce.
Carefully layer vegetables in pattern.
Season with herbs, salt, and pepper.
Cover and cook slowly for 30 minutes.

SMELLS OF CHILDHOOD
